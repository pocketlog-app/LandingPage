Private by design

Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device.
Pocketlog is built for the follow-up — not for tracking.

Overview

Pocketlog is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal information. All content you create within the app remains on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

Pocketlog does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Contact lists
  • Location data
  • Messages or notes you create
  • Usage tied to your identity
  • Advertising identifiers

Diagnostics & Crash Data

To improve reliability and performance, Pocketlog may receive limited diagnostic information provided by Apple, including:

  • Crash logs
  • Performance metrics
  • Anonymized usage statistics

This information does not identify you personally and cannot be used to track you across apps or services.

Data Storage

All notes, logs, and content you create are stored locally on your device. Pocketlog does not operate a backend server and does not transmit your content externally.

Third-Party Services

Pocketlog does not use third-party analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

If you choose to share diagnostics with Apple, that data is governed by Apple’s privacy policies.

Your Privacy Rights

Because Pocketlog does not collect personal data, we do not maintain user accounts or personal records. If you uninstall the app, your data is removed from your device in accordance with your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

Pocketlog does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change, this policy will be updated and the revision date will reflect those changes.
